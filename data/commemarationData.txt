Our righteous Father Nicholas the Monk, the former soldier.
The Holy Martyr Achalcus.
Our righteous Father Antiochus of Mar Sabbas Monastery.
Our righteous Father Bitimion of Scete.
Our righteous Father Aphrodisius of Palestine.
The Holy New Martyr Ahmet the Calligrapher of Constantinople.
Saint Delphinus, Bishop of Bordeaux.
The Holy New Hieromartyr Innocent, Archimandrite in Voronezh.
+DECEMBER 25
+Our Lord and God and Saviour Jesus Christ, Whose Nativity according to the flesh we celebrate.
The Magi who journeyed from Persia and worshipped the Lord.
+ The Shepherds abiding in the field who beheld the Lord.

+DECEMBER 26
+ Our Most Holy Lady the Theotokos, whose Synaxis we celebrate, and whose Flight into Egypt we commemorate.
+ Our Father among the Saints Euthymius the Confessor, Bishop of Sardis.
+ Our righteous Father Constantine of Synnada in Phrygia, a Jew who believed in Christ.
+ Our righteous Father Evarestus the Studite.
+ The Holy New Hieromartyr Constantine the Russian, who contested in Constantinople.
+The Holy Martyrs Jonas and the 50 Monks and 65 Laymen who were martyred by the Lutherans in Pechenga.
Our righteous Father Nicodemus of Tisman.
The Holy New Hieromartyr Andrew, Bishop of Ufa.
